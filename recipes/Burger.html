Cheese Burger

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef (80/20 is ideal)
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 slice of cheese (cheddar, American, or your choice)
  • 1 hamburger Bun
  • Optional toppings: lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, ketchup, mustard

Instructions

  1. Form the Patty: Divide the ground beef into two equal portions and shape them into patties, about 3/4 inch thick. Make a slight indentation in the center of each patty (this helps them cook evenly). Season both sides with salt and pepper.
  2. Cook the Patty: Heat a skillet or grill over medium-high heat. Cook the patties for about 3-4 minutes on one side. Flip them and cook for another 2-3 minutes. Place the cheese slice on top of the patty during the last minute of cooking to melt.
  3. Toast the Bun (Optional): While the cheese melts, you can toast the hamburger bun in the same skillet for a minute or two until golden.
  4. Assemble: Place the cooked patty on the bottom half of the bun. Add your favorite toppings (lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, ketchup, mustard).
  5. Serve: Place the top half of the bun on the burger and enjoy!

Feel free to customize with any toppings you like!
