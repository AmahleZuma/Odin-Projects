PB and J Sandwich

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread (your choice)
  • 2 tablespoons peanut butter
  • 2 tablespoons jelly or jam (your favorite flavor)

Instructions

  1. Spread the Peanut Butter: Take one slice of bread and spread the peanut butter evenly over it.
  2. Add the Jelly: On the second slice of bread, spread the jelly or jam.
  3. Combine: Place the slice with jelly on top of the slice with peanut butter, jelly side down.
  4. Slice (Optional): Cut the sandwich in half or into quarters if you prefer.
  5. Enjoy! Serve immediately or pack it for later.

Feel free to add banana slices or honey for an extra twist!
