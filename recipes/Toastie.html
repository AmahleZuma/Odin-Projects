Cheese And Tomato Toastie

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread (your choice)
  • 1-2 slices of cheese (cheddar, mozzarella, or your favorite)
  • 1 small tomato, sliced
  • Butter or margarine (for spreading)
  • Salt and pepper (optional)

Instructions

  1. Prep the Bread: Spread butter on one side of each slice of bread.
  2. Assemble: Place one slice of bread, butter-side down, in a frying pan. Layer with cheese and tomato slices. Sprinkle with a little salt and pepper if desired. Top with the second slice of bread, butter-side up.
  3. Cook: Heat the pan over medium heat. Cook for about 3-4 minutes until the bottom is golden brown. Carefully flip the toastie and cook for another 3-4 minutes, until the other side is golden and the cheese is melted.
  4. Serve: Remove from the pan, cut in half, and enjoy your delicious toastie!
